Badass Marshmallows Who Fall Hard. Fast.

Macy West writes fun, flirty, feel-good romances where big, burly alpha men fall hard for the sassy women who steal their hearts—and sometimes their shirts. Mountain men, ex-military protectors, bikers, and the occasional cowboy, all brought to their knees by the strong women they're destined to love.

Welcome to Wild Rose Mountain, where rescue dogs often give love a nudge, an outlaw motorcycle club is up to no good, and the alpha heroes are about to discover that yoga pants might just be their undoing. Every story is fun, flirty, and steeped in heart, with a satisfying dash of steam.

Always SAFE. No cheating, no cliffhangers, just a heartwarming HEA every time.

If you're craving small-town sparks, swoony alphas, and happily-ever-afters that hit just right, you've found your next favorite escape.
