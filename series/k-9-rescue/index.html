Wild Rose Mountain K-9 Rescue

Welcome to Wild Rose Mountain K-9 Rescue in West Texas! Where the ex-military alpha mountain men are hot, over the top, and about to be brought to their knees by the sassy, strong women who cross their paths.

ALL books feature protective, ex-military, badass marshmallow alpha heroes; strong and sassy heroines; fun and witty banter; heartfelt, emotional journeys; and earned HEAs. Read on for some of the differences...
