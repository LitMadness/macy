About Macy

Macy West writes flirty, feel-good romances where big, burly alpha men fall hard and fast for the sassy heroines who steal their hearts (and sometimes their shirts). Expect mountain men, ex-military protectors, bikers and the occasional cowboy, all brought to their knees by the strong women they’re destined to love.

An award-winning author in another genre, Macy now crafts spicy short reads full of wit, warmth, and wildly satisfying HEAs.

She lives in the mountains with her own bearded hero and their personal zoo of cats, dogs and the biggest, baddest dust bunnies this side of the Pecos.
