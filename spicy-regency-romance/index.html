Spicy Regency Romance

 

Love your alpha heroes with a side of cravats and candlelight? When Macy isn't writing her modern-day, badass marshmallow alphas, she's stepping back two centuries as Larissa Lyons—and trading bearded heroes for breeches, without losing a single ounce of the wit, warmth, and swoon you came here for.

Larissa's steamy Regencies are a little literary, a lot of fun, and full of the same banter, heart, and happily-ever-afters you love from Macy—just dressed up in the rich, authentic language of 1815. Think Jane Austen with more heat, or the beloved Signet Regencies of Mary Balogh, Carla Kelly, and Barbara Metzger, reimagined for today's readers who like their history with some sizzle and witty wordplay.
